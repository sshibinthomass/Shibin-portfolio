Yoga your way. Anywhere, anytime.

On your smartphone, you can download the NextGen App and discover a high-end yoga experience. Simply place your phone in the given slot in our headset and experience new realities with complete immersion during yoga.It stands true that VR devices poses risk to eyes and harmful light rays which arise from the mobile screens may affect the eyes of the User with serious complications. To avoid this, we have designed a customized PROTECTIVE FILTER SCREENS. This Light Rays Protective Filter (LRPF) Screen is placed on the either side of the biconvex lens. One LRPF Screen is placed posteriorly and the other one anteriorly.A customized Virtual Reality(VR) device is made with innovative features to improve concentration while doing Yoga The novel VR wearable device is 3D printed and a special biconvex lens and a headphone is attached to it. A smart phone used by the individual shall be fixed to the wearable device to provide a VR experience.

  • Lightweight and durable Headset - designed with flexible material and cotton cushion padding.
  • Next Gen Lenses - offering a wide field of view with significantly reduced glare for crystal-clear HD optics.
  • LRPF filters, Inter-pupillary distance adjuster - provides eye protection and drastically improves vision clarity.
  • Inbuilt Spatial Audio - discover a new dimension in sound through our integrated speakers.
  • Pulse rate monitoring - keep check of your pulse and temperature.
  • Controller-less VR - no additional devices, such as controllers or wires, required.
  • Interactive Virtual Tutor - to assist you, with voice, through your yoga experience.
  • Gaze interaction - move through VR application with just your eyes.
  • Gyroscopic walk - with just a 30’ head tilt to walk through the environment and experience a safe, Total Virtual Reality of 360’.
  • 3D Virtual Buttons - for easy interaction and control with VR environment and characters.
  • Simple UI - to ensure a user-friendly experience for all ages.

From the Manufacturer

  • 3D printed headset, designed using ABS (acrylonitrile-butadlene-styrene) material to specifically ensure a lightweight and durable product to avoid extra weight on your head and to prevent any breakage incase it falls.
    • Material Prototyping
  • Using ABS significantly reduces the manufacturing cost, and hence the final product is highly economic.
  • ABS filament’s properties of higher temperature resistance, flexibility, machinability and strength make it a preference for engineers and others where mechanical uses are important.
  • The time it takes to print the entire object is less than a day.
  • Chemical compositions: 20% acrylonitrile, 25% butadiene and 55% styrene.
    • Pulse-rate Monitoring Unit
  • Using Particle-photon to real-time monitor user’s blood pressure level.
  • With access to cloud data, users can view body parameters at any time on their mobile screen.
  • Particle photon - IoT kit with powerful 120Mhz ARM Cortex M3 microcontroller and inbuilt Broadcom Wi-Fi chip in a tiny thumbnail-sized module, access to a free cloud service of the particle cloud.
    • Next Gen Lenses
  • Long hours exposure to VR can cause severe strain on the eyes and headaches. To curb this, we have incorporated a customised Light Ray Protective Filter (LRPF) on both sides of the lens.
  • This protective filter screen would ease your eye-vision and significantly reduce harmful rays directly hitting your eye.
    • Power Lenses
  • Next Gen Power lens made for use in your VR headset.
  • These lenses are tailored to your prescription to make your visual experience clear and comfortable.
  • Your custom-made lenses are of high-quality with anti-glare coating and protective filter screens.
