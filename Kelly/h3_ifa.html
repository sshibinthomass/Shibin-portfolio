Project Description:

NxtGen aims to provide new exposure and conscience in learning through AR/VR/XR. The project is developed as an Android Application which can be used to enable users learn in an immersive way. The users are exposed to the mixture of virtual reality through different context. These technologies are used to address and pitch solution for difficulty faced by learners in learning process and provide gamified learning content. Technological advances in Virtual Reality (VR) and Augmented Reality (AR) offer new immersive training systems through XR. These technologies can be adapted by organizations to boost employee retention as well as give new trainees access to real-world scenarios in a safe digital environment. Training can be undertaken through the application. Even complex and intricate training simulations which are in unsafe environments in physical world can be enabled in safe digital environments, thus improving the overall training standards. On the other hand, this application helps young learners to increase their understanding content through immersive 3D visual experience. Learning a concept visually can increase the motivational asset of the learners. It kindles the curiosity in their minds and hearts to learn more effectively. Complex concepts can be simplified through 3D animated and interactive visuals. Thus, through these domains the knowledge domain of the learners is increased. While learning the learners are exposed to Fun, Challenge and Curiosity. The gamified learning program is designed in categorized modules in order to suit real time usage in any environment with external motion controllers being optional.

INNOVATION
